Burger Recipe

Description

Chris' Bay area burger which is the hit in the hometown of a guy who posted this recipe on allrecipes.com

The recipe is simple and straightforward.

The ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon sail
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 4 hamburger buns, split
  • 1 fress tomato
  • Salad leaves
  • Cheese optional

Step by Step guide

  1. Mix together the ground beef, garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper, and basil.
  2. Divide meat into four balls, and flatten into patties.
  3. Cook the patties for about 3 to 5 minutes on each side.
  4. Put the patties onto the slide bum.
  5. put desired ingridient on top of the patties.
  6. Cover with another bum.
  7. Enjoy your burgers.
