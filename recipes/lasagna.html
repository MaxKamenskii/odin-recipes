Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is the world's best lasagna accoridng to allrecipes.com although I found the recipe kind of weak and simple.

The ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato and tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Step by Step guide

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
