Christmas Cookies Recipe

Description

Chocolate crinkle cookies have become a holiday staple, but they're also great any time of year. They're cake-like cookies made with unsweetened cocoa powder, vegetable oil, and a handful of other pantry staple ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 1 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 4 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla flour
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup confectioners' sugar

Step by Step Guide

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Mix sugar, cocoa, and oil together in a bowl.
  3. Beat in eggs, one at a time, until combined. Stir in vanilla.
  4. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in another bowl. Gradually stir flour mixture into the cocoa mixture until thoroughly mixed. Cover dough and regrigerate for at least 4 hours.
  5. Preheat the oven to 350 degree F(175 degrees C).
  6. Line two cookie sheets with parchment paper. Rool or scoop chilled dought into 1-inch balls. Coat each ball in confectioners' sugar and place 1 inch apart on the prepared cookie sheets.
  7. Bake in the preheated over for 10 to 12 minutes. Let stand on the cookie sheet for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks to cool.
  8. Repeat step 7 to make the remaining batches.
  9. Enjoy!
